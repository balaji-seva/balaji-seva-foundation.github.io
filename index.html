Preethadhipati Sri Sri Sri Narayananda Swamiji, of the Balaji Matha temple, San Jose has founded “Balaji Seva Foundation” with a mission to empower change.

  • In San Jose, USA - To provide spiritual and mental support to the local communities. Additionally, donate food locally to the needy and supplies and monetary help to the local schools.

  • In Devanhalli, Bangalore, India - To build a school of Vedic learning along with a state-approved curriculum for the indigent local children. To construct an old age home and a health clinic for the underprivileged of our society.

Phase 1 of the mission for India

Start construction of the school with 25 rooms for study, a kitchen, a hostel, a library, and a dining hall in April’23. The land is already procured to start phase 1, and the estimated duration of the project is 18-24 months at the cost of $500,000.

The school will make a big difference in the lives of underprivileged kids in areas around Devanhalli, Bangalore, India.
